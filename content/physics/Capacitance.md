title: "Capacitance"
---
- definitions
	- theoretical :: $\Delta V=\frac{Q}{C}$
	- physical :: $C=\frac{\kappa\epsilon_0A}{d}$
- potential energy :: $U_C=\frac{1}{2}Q\Delta V=\frac{1}{2}C(\Delta V)^2$
## Capacitors
- capacitors in series :: $\frac{1}{C_s}=\sum_i\frac{1}{C_i}$
- capacitors in parallel :: $C_p=\sum_{i}^{} C_i$
